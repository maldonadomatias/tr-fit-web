| D4 | **Shoulders/Chest**: Hombros (Press Militar 6a8) + Pecho Superior + rear delts |

- 1 leg day → **3 upper days + 1 leg = upper-biased**. The 4th day is a
  shoulder/upper-chest specialization (men's emphasis = delts/chest).
- 9-10 slots/day. M1-M5 identical.
