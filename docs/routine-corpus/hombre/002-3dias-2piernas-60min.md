## Split = leg-priority hybrid (NOT PPL)
| Day | Block |
|-----|-------|
| D1 | Cuádriceps (Sentadilla 6a8) + Pantorrilla + **Espalda** (Jalón 8) |
| D2 | Pecho (Press Plano 6a8) + Biceps + Triceps (push+arms) |
| D3 | Femoral (Peso Muerto 6a8) + Aductores + **Hombros** (Press Militar 6a8) |

- Same 3 days, but choosing 2 leg days **reshapes the whole split**: legs split
  quad-day (D1) / ham-day (D3), upper crammed into the gaps. Mirrors the women
  3-day-ish lower split.
- 2 warmups on D1 & D3 (leg→upper transition). 9 slots/day.
